WOOECOMMERCE

This project focused on improving the UI/UX of a WooCommerce-based e-commerce platform. The goal was to enhance user navigation, streamline the checkout process, and optimize the design for mobile users. My role involved conducting user research, wireframing, prototyping, and creating a visually appealing, accessible design. Key improvements included a simplified product discovery process, faster checkout, and a responsive layout. The result was a user-friendly e-commerce experience tailored to the target audience, showcasing my ability to create functional and engaging designs.
